How to Make Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g of spaghetti
  • 150g of pancetta or bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g of Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a pan over medium heat until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  5. Remove the pan from heat, add the pasta, then quickly pour in the egg mixture and toss well.
  6. Add pasta water a little at a time to get a creamy consistency.
